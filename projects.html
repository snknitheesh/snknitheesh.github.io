Disaster Management using Pololu3PI+

I'm thrilled to share my latest project, where I've programmed the Pololu Corporation Pololu 3pi+ robot using the Arduino IDE to simulate a disaster management scenario. The robot's mission is to locate magnets, which represent injured individuals, and follow a predefined workflow to indicate their positions.
